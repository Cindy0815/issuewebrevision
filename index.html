Our Mission

Working to support and promote shelters, rescue groups, pet adoption, and no-kill animal rescue in New York City.

Challenges Unique to Shelter

Overcrowding
Mandatory holding periods
Limited resources

Approximately 8-12 million companion animals enter animal shelters every year and approximately 5-9 million are euthanized
Only 25 percent of dogs and 24 percent of cats that enter animal shelters are adopted
Only 10% of the animals received by shelters have been spayed or neutered. 75% of owned pets are neutered

You can make a difference

Put a stop to animal cruelty.
Choose adoption to support local animal shelters.

By adopting a pet from an animal shelter or rescue group, you'll help save the lives of two animals: the pet you adopt and a homeless animal that can be rescued because of space you make available.
